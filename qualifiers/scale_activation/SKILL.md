# Scale-activation qualifier

## When this handles a lead
An account that just activated the Scale plan and carries a high lead score.

## How it works
Account-first. Read the CRM record, pull usage and the internal user roster, run buying-group discovery (enrich the roster, segment by role), enrich the company, and map live use cases. Judge holistically with the chosen target taken from the candidate set.

## How to judge
They have committed; the goal is to ensure they get value and expand. Weight what the Scale activation unlocks and who owns it.

## How to draft
Lead with what the Scale plan unlocks for their specific use case and how to get full value from it, anchored on a grounded usage or activation fact. Offer onboarding/expansion help. Soft close with an offer to talk through their rollout.

## How to follow up
Two nudges (5 days, then 10), drafts only, shorter each time. Follow-up 1: a concrete step to get more from Scale. Follow-up 2: a soft check-in. A reply stops the sequence.

## Notes
The account is the unit. Outreach can correctly go to a different person than the named lead; the disposition records the chosen target and why.
