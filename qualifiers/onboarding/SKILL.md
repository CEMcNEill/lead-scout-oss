# Onboarding qualifier

## When this handles a lead
A newly assigned customer to onboard and activate. The named contact is the
person to help, so the work is person-centered but usage-aware.

## How it works
1. Read the CRM record for ground truth.
2. Enrich the named contact for role and likely pain.
3. Pull usage for activation signals: what the customer has and has not yet
   adopted.
4. Map what the customer is trying to do from the usage and the persona.
5. Judge holistically. Target is the named contact.
6. If the disposition is call, draft an activation-led message that leads with
   the use case they are working toward and the next step that unblocks it.

## Notes
Onboarding outreach is help, not a pitch. The draft leads with the customer's
own goal as the dossier shows it, grounded in real activation signals.
