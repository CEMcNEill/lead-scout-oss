# Spend-spike qualifier

## When this handles a lead
A paying account whose spend is accelerating: MRR over $1k with a forecasted >50% increase this month.

## How it works
Account-first. Read the CRM record, pull usage and the internal user roster, run buying-group discovery (enrich the roster, segment by role), enrich the company, and map live use cases. Judge holistically with the chosen target taken from the candidate set.

## How to judge
The trajectory is the signal. Weight the rate of change and whether the growth implies a use case worth a conversation right now.

## How to draft
Lead with what their accelerating usage suggests they are building, and offer to help them get ahead of it (right plan, cost controls, the products that fit the new workload). Anchor on the grounded trajectory. Helpful and timely, never alarmist about the spend. Soft close.

## How to follow up
Two timely nudges (3 days, then 7), drafts only, shorter each time. Follow-up 1: a concrete cost-control or right-plan step. Follow-up 2: a soft check-in. A reply stops the sequence.

## Notes
The account is the unit. Outreach can correctly go to a different person than the named lead; the disposition records the chosen target and why.
