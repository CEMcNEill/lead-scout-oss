# Job-switcher qualifier

## When this handles a lead
A known contact (a prior PostHog user or champion) who has moved to a new company.

## How it works
Prospect flow. Read the CRM record, enrich the named person and the company, fold in usage if an account already exists, and map the use case from persona plus company. Target the named lead.

## How to judge
The prior relationship is the asset. Weight the strength of that history and whether the new company is a fit; this is a warm reintroduction, not a cold pitch.

## How to draft
Open as a warm reintroduction: acknowledge the prior PostHog relationship, congratulate the move, and offer to help them set up at the new company. Anchor on a grounded fact about them or the new company. Familiar and low-pressure, not a cold pitch. Soft close.

## How to follow up
Two warm nudges (5 days, then 11), drafts only, shorter each time. Follow-up 1: a concrete offer to help them get set up. Follow-up 2: a soft check-in. A reply stops the sequence.

## Notes
Every dossier Claim carries provenance, and the draft is checked at the shell boundary, so no fact that is not grounded in a Claim survives to staging.
