# Engineering-headcount-growth qualifier

## When this handles a lead
A product-led account whose engineering headcount is growing.

## How it works
Prospect flow. Read the CRM record, enrich the named person and the company, fold in usage if an account already exists, and map the use case from persona plus company. Target the named lead.

## How to judge
Growth implies new tooling and collaboration needs. Usage may be thin; weight the growth signal and company fit, and lead with what a scaling engineering team needs.

## How to draft
Lead with what a fast-growing engineering team needs (visibility, collaboration, the products that fit) tied to a grounded growth signal. Speak to the named lead's role. Soft close offering to share how similar teams set up PostHog.

## How to follow up
Two light nudges (6 days, then 12), drafts only, shorter each time. Follow-up 1: a specific tooling angle for a growing team. Follow-up 2: a soft check-in. A reply stops the sequence.

## Notes
Every dossier Claim carries provenance, and the draft is checked at the shell boundary, so no fact that is not grounded in a Claim survives to staging.
