# Recent-fundraise qualifier

## When this handles a lead
A product-led account whose company recently raised funding.

## How it works
Prospect flow. Read the CRM record, enrich the named person and the company, fold in usage if an account already exists, and map the use case from persona plus company. Target the named lead.

## How to judge
New budget and scaling pressure make this a timing play. Usage may be thin; weight company fit and the scaling need the raise implies over current activity.

## Notes
Every dossier Claim carries provenance, and the draft is checked at the shell boundary, so no fact that is not grounded in a Claim survives to staging.
