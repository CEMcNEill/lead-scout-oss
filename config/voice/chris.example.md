# Voice profile (template)

Seed copy. On install, setup copies this to `config/voice/chris.md`, which is the
active profile the drafter loads on every run. Your real profile is local only and
never committed: it is learned from your own sent mail by the `voice-calibration`
skill (run "train my voice" to bootstrap, then "review my voice profile" to accept
rules). The style rules below are the compliance floor and always win.

## Tone
- Plain, direct, human. Write like a person who has done the homework, not a
  template.
- No hype, no buzzwords, no "I hope this finds you well."
- Lead with the recipient's situation, not with us.

## Structure
- Short. A few sentences. One clear reason for reaching out.
- Open with the specific use case or pain the evidence shows.
- One soft, concrete next step. No hard ask.

## Style rules (compliance floor)
- Plain prose. No emdashes. No bold for emphasis.
- Links are fine when each one maps to the recipient's job. No generic feature
  enumeration.
- Reference only what the dossier supports. Never guess at facts.

## Do not
- Do not flatter.
- Do not claim to know things the evidence does not show.
- Do not stack multiple asks.
